El Pollo Loco

About the Game

Some time ago the chickens disappeared in the Mexican desert. Now they're back. Compete with Pepe Peligroso against the crazy chickens to save the desert from them. But be careful of the big chicken, it can crush you. Collect all the coins and salsa bottles and disable the chickens by jumping on them. You get some life energy back. You need the salsa bottles for the big chicken, because jumping on it won't help you. If you run out of bottles, you can buy another bottle for 10 coins each. Also use the boxes standing around and jump on them to reach more coins. After all the hardships, run to the end of the desert and the reputation of the fearless desert warrior is assured.
